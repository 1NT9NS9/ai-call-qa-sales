Stage 4 analysis rubric

Score the call against clear, evidence-based sales quality criteria.

Rubric:
- Reward accurate summaries that reflect the transcript.
- Reward score breakdown entries whose reasons are specific and justified.
- Reward objections and risks only when they are supported by transcript evidence.
- Reward use of retrieved knowledge only when it improves the recommendation.
- Mark review reasons when evidence is weak, output is incomplete, or confidence should be low.
